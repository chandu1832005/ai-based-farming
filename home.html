About Smart Farming

Smart farming leverages AI to tackle agriculture’s biggest challenges, optimizing crop selection, yield prediction, and resource management. Through data-driven methods, machine learning and deep learning models provide actionable insights for farmers.

Crop Selection

Utilizes machine learning algorithms for crop selection based on soil compatibility and environmental factors.

Yield Prediction

Forecasting crop production using deep learning models to ensure food sufficiency for a growing population.

Soil Classification

Classification of soil fertility and compatibility using data-driven machine learning methods.

Water Management

Efficient water resource management by predicting crop water needs based on environmental data.

Technologies in Use

This platform leverages various algorithms for enhancing agriculture:

  • Machine Learning: Algorithms like Random Forest, SVM, and KNN for crop classification and yield prediction.
  • Deep Learning: Techniques like CNN and RNN for analyzing complex datasets and time-series data.
  • Time Series Analysis: ARIMA and other models for predicting commodity prices and crop demand.
